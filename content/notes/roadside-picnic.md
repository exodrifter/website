# Roadside Picnic

**Roadside Picnic** is a novel written by [Arkady Strugatsky](../notes/arkady-strugatsky.md) and [Boris Strugatsky](../notes/boris-strugatsky.md).

It was adapted to a movie in [Stalker (1979)](stalker.md). [^1]
