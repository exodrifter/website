**Pepcorn** is a recipe by Brian David Gilbert.

Ingredients:
- 1/2c Popcorn Kernels
- 1/2 tsp theater salt
- 3 tbsp Vegetable Oil
- 2-3 tbsp Lao Gan Ma spicy chili crisp
- Fine red pepper flakes, to taste (optional)
